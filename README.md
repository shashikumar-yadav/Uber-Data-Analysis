
# Uber Data Analysis

### Case Study: Solving the Uber Ride Cancellation Crisis

The Story:

You are a Data Scientist at Uber. Your Product Manager walks in on Monday morning and says:

"Riders are furious. They book a cab, but either the driver cancels or the app says 'No Cars Available' — especially for airport trips. We are losing thousands of rides every week.

Your job: find out WHY and tell us what to do about it."

## Think before we jump on Analysis:

#### Before opening any data, a good analyst asks clarifying questions first.

### -----------------------------------------------------------

❓ 1. Question
Is this happening at specific times of day?	

🎯 Why it matters
Could be a rush-hour problem
### -----------------------------------------------------------

❓ 2. Question
Is it City→Airport, Airport→City, or both?	

🎯 Why it matters
Different routes may need different fixes
### -----------------------------------------------------------


❓ 3. Question
Is the failure 'Cancelled' or 'No Cars Available'?	

🎯 Why it matters
These are completely different root causes
### -----------------------------------------------------------


❓ 4. Question
Has anything changed recently (incentives, app updates)?	

🎯 Why it matters
Could point to a single trigger
### -----------------------------------------------------------
